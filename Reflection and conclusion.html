Reflection and Conclusion






From this project I can conclude that in different parts of the world, different systems of waste management are used. Each system has its perks and advantages. The problem of managing waste especially plastics is a problem in the entire world. Together, we have to battle this problem. We have to follow the 3 r's: reduce, reuse and recycle. Limit the use of plastic. Push in our society for a need to segregate waste. We cannot rely only on our government for the proper disposal of our waste. We need to take proactive steps ourelves too.
